PIOI (Polytechnic-ITE Olympiad in Informatics) is an event that has been started this year aiming to give polytechnic and ITE students the experience of participating in an informatics olympiad. In doing so, we hope that other students will learn about programming focused on not just building software, but also writing efficient programs.
